Papa Josh's Tasty Wings

Traditional Buffalo Chicken Wings

First you bread the chicken wings thickly, then fry them quickly.

Ingredients

  • 10 juicy wings
  • 2 eggs
  • flour
  • panko
  • buffalo sauce
  • 1qt oil
  1. Dip the wings in the beat up eggs
  2. Dip the wings in the flour
  3. Dip the wings in the panko
  4. Fry in hot oil for 10 minutes. Caution HOT!
  5. Shake wings in the sauce
  6. Enjoy!
