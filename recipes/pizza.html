Papa Josh's Tasty Pizza

That's a tasty lookin' pie

There's nobody who can cook pizza as well as outlined in this recipe! Not even Papa John himself

Ingredients

  • Pre-made dough
  • Shredded mozzerella
  • Thin pepperoni
  • Pre-made pizza sauce
  1. Spin the dough into a 20 inch circle
  2. Top with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni in any order you'd like
  3. Bake for 20 minutes
